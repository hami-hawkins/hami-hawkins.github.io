Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to understand and manage our thoughts and feelings, and how we can manage them in relation to ourselves and to others. It allows us to understand the feelings of those around us, aiding us to relate to them and hence strengthens those relationships through a mutual understanding of any obstacles before us.

How is EQ different to IQ?

While IQ largely deals with our ability to process information and problem-solve, EQ is more applicable to our mental wellbeing. Why is EQ important?

EQ helps us connect with our work colleagues, friends, family, strangers, etc. The old adage rings true here - if you want to go fast go alone, but if you want to go far go together. By being able to understand how one another feels about different things, we are better able to navigate any conflict which may arise, we can build stronger relationships to those around us, and we can better grapple with our own internal struggles that we face each day.
